About

Short version.

Junior at Colby, double major in Computer Science and Science, Technology & Society. I got into CS because I wanted to build things; I stayed for the part where you ask who it’s for and why it matters.

I grew up Bangladeshi in the Bronx, so I’m used to moving between worlds. These days that shows up in how I think about product, strategy, and who’s in the room when we make decisions.

Right now I’m most interested in product management, consulting, and technical strategy—anything where I can help turn messy problems into stuff that actually works for people.
